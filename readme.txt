No measurement = No management


In today's world one person poses multiple payment methods. Multiple payment methods makes it easy to spend money but it makes difficult to keep track of small expenses in daily life.
At the end of the time this small and regular subscriptions makes large amount. Very few people keeps track of this regularly.


objective of this personal project is to bring all expenses in consideration then visualize in a power BI dashboard, so that one can take control over his avoidable expenses. 

it takes my bank statement(s), mastercard invoice pdf file as input then creates dataset for power BI.
